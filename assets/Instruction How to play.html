Directions

  • Press any key to start the game.
  • Think of a capital city of a Country.
  • Or, you can press "hint" button which shows a country's name then you can guess its capital city.
  • Type the capital city.
  • You have ten letters guess to get to the capital city you have in mined .
  • If you get the capital city with the right spelling you will get a point which you can see next to "WIN" on the top right cornner.
    • If you didn't get the right answer after the ten letters guess , you will be prompted by the "Press any key to get started! "

  • If you enter ten wrong capital cities consequetively game will be over.

    • GOOD LUCK!
